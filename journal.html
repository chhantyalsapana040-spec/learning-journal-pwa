Weekly Journal

Week 1: Temperature Converter PWA

The focus was building a simple PWA that converts between Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin. I learned to design a basic app layout and use input fields with real-time updates. The challenge here was getting the conversion logic to work correctly, ensuring responsive design and deploying on PythonAnywhere.

Wek 2: Learning Journal PWA

How did you approach mobile-first design?
I began by creating the layout of smaller screens initially to ensure that all was readable and understandable on mobile. After it was fine there I put in media queries in order to modify the layout to bigger screens such as tablets and desktop.

What was the most useful HTML or CSS concept you applied this week?
The most beneficial concept was flexbox. It also simplified the process of aligning things and developing a clean navigation bar and made the layout to be flexible across multiple screen sizes.

What part of HTML or CSS did you find most challenging or confusing?
The hardest task was maintaining the distance and laying of both mobile and desktop screens. The slightest modification of padding or margins tended to have a different impact on each device.
